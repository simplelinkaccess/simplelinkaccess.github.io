Guide 6

Manage Your Subscriptions

Your subscriptions are managed directly from the SimpleLinkAccess bot.

Open @simplelinkaccessbot and press My Configs. You will see a list of all your subscriptions.

What you will see

  • Subscription name
  • Start date
  • Expiration date
  • Extend button

This makes it easy to see which subscriptions are active and which ones have already expired.

Extend a subscription

If a subscription expires, you do not need to create a new one. Press Extend below the subscription you want to continue using.

Your subscription will be extended, and you can keep using the same subscription in Hiddify. There is no need to import it again.

Multiple subscriptions

You can have more than one subscription. Some people prefer separate subscriptions for different purposes. Others need more daily traffic than a single subscription provides. Every subscription is managed independently and appears separately in My Configs.

Good to know: Hiddify stores your subscription locally. Once you have imported it, you normally will not need to import it again after renewing.
