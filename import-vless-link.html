Guide 5

Import Your VLESS Link

Once Hiddify is installed, configured and your subscription is ready, importing takes less than a minute.

Option 1: QR code

Inside @simplelinkaccessbot, open My Configs and display the QR code.

  1. In Hiddify, press the plus button.
  2. Choose Scan QR Code.
  3. Scan the code.

Your subscription will be imported automatically.

Option 2: subscription link

If you are using a desktop computer, copying the subscription link is usually more convenient.

  1. In the bot, open My Configs.
  2. Choose Copy Subscription Link.
  3. In Hiddify, press the plus button.
  4. Choose Add from Clipboard or Import from Link.

Keep your subscription up to date

Hiddify remembers your subscription. When your provider updates servers or configuration, press the refresh button in Hiddify. There is no need to import your subscription again.

You are done. Your internet access is now ready.
