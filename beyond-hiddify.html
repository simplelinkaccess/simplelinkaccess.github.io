Beyond Hiddify

Hiddify is our recommendation, but your VLESS subscription is not tied to a specific application.

If you already have a favorite client, you can continue using it. Here are a few excellent alternatives.

A

v2rayNG

One of the most popular Android clients for VLESS and Xray-based protocols. Recommended for experienced Android users.

 B

sing-box

A powerful multi-platform client with excellent flexibility and support for modern protocols.

 C

Streisand

A modern client for iPhone and iPad with native support for VLESS subscriptions.

Why we still recommend Hiddify

Not because it is the only option. We recommend Hiddify because it offers the best balance between simplicity, features and cross-platform support.

If Hiddify works well for you, there is no reason to switch. If another client better fits your workflow, your VLESS subscription will work there too.

Open technologies are about freedom of choice. That is why SimpleLinkAccess is built around open standards instead of a proprietary application.
